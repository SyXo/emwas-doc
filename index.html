Efficient Masturbation With Aggregated Search v1.0

emwas is a meta search engine that gather searches from different porn sites and display them into one only convivial interface. It allows you to find the porn you're looking for faster and gives you the direct link to the original videos. It is for now intended to work with a limited number of non-free big porn websites

link https://api.emwas.co

get /search

 

Performs a search on all the services and get a list of videos from that.

Parameters chevron_right expand_more

ParameterTypeDescription
Query
q*string

The free text search you would type on your porn site search engine. All porn site handle very well pornstar names. Some also finds pretty well if you type a category or something else.

curl -X GET \
  https://api.emwas.co/search?q=<value>

200 OK chevron_right expand_more

application/json

application/json

ParameterTypeDescription
service*integer

The service this video comes from (refer to /services for more info)

title*string

The title of the scene.

url*uri (string)

The url of the scene on its original website.

thumbnailUrlarray [uri (string)]

The list of thumbnail for the scenes on the original website.

performersarray (string)

The list of the performer names declared on the scene by the original website. Guys are sometimes not declared by the sites (which is a pity), so you won't always have them.

descriptionstring

The description of the scene by the original website. Might be very long depending on the service and the scene.

date*date

The date the scene was posted. The format is the standard Javascript ISO8601 format.

lengthinteger

The length of the video in seconds. Might not be extremely precise depending on the website since some of them display video length in minutes.
